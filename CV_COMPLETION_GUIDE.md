## 🔍 How to Gather This Information

### From LinkedIn:
1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
2. Click on "Experience" section
3. For each position, note:
   - Exact job titles and dates
   - Company descriptions
   - Your responsibilities and achievements
   - Technologies mentioned

### From Your Memory/Records:
1. Think about specific projects you've worked on
2. List all technologies you've used professionally
3. Recall any achievements or recognition you've received
4. Note any leadership or mentoring experience

### From Colleagues/Managers:
1. Ask for feedback on your key contributions
2. Get specific examples of your impact
3. Ask about technologies you've used that you might have forgotten

## 💡 Pro Tips

- **Be Specific**: Instead of "worked on web applications," say "developed React-based e-commerce platform serving 10,000+ users"
- **Use Action Verbs**: Started, Led, Developed, Implemented, Optimized, etc.
- **Quantify Results**: "Improved performance by 40%" is better than "improved performance"
- **Tailor Content**: Adjust your CV for different types of positions
- **Keep It Current**: Update regularly as you gain new skills and experience
